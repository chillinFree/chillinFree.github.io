Research Goal

My long-term research goal is to develop robots with human-level versatility in perception and reasoning, ultimately enhancing everyday life at scale.
My current focus lies at the intersection of robotics and machine learning, particularly in building general-purpose manipulation systems that can robustly operate in unstructured real-world environments.
