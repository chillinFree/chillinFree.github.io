I am an Undergraduate at DCST, Tsinghua University. I am currently working at Tsinghua Embodied AI Lab (TEA Lab) affiliated to Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University under the supervision of Prof. Huazhe Xu.

Research Interest
I am interested in embodied AI, especially bimanual dexterous manipulation. My recent research focuses on creating a more generalizable and data-efficient robot learning methods and systems for manipulation.
